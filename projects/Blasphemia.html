"Blasphemia"

“What is hidden in a monastery ruled not by God and faith, but by fear and sin?”

“Quid celat monasterium, quod Non A Deo et fide regitur, sed per timorem et peccatum?”

Concept & Theme

The player takes on the role of a young nun who has recently arrived at a remote monastery. As the player explores the monastery, they discover:

  • Strange symbols, hidden rooms, diaries, and notes;
  • The frightening behavior of the abbesses and ritual objects.
  • The disappearance of other novices.
  • A recruitment system for an ancient cult that exists covertly under the guise of a monastic community.
This page and project are still under development.
